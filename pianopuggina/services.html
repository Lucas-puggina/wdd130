IMMUNIZATION:

Another very common, however complicated problem with pianos are the termites. They are like cancer on your instrument, if you don't realize in time, maybe you have already lost your piano. Immunization is a challenging work because we need to track the termites until reach the queen, because in other way they will never really leave your instrument. Through a lot of trying we develop our own way to cure your instrument from this cancer.
